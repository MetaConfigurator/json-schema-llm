The schema describes a Todo. It is an object type. The main properties of the schema are: 'id' is a string, the unique identifier for a todo. It is optional. 'title' is a string, the title of the todo. It is required and must have a minimum length of 1 and a maximum length of 255. 'status' is a string, representing whether the task has been completed. It is required and has an enum of allowed values: '1', '2'. 'status_name' is a string, the name of the status. It is optional and has a maximum length of 255. 'created' is a string, the date and time that the todo was created. It is required and must be in datetime format. 'updated' is a string, the date and time that the todo was last modified. It is required and must also be in datetime format. No additional properties are allowed at the root level.
