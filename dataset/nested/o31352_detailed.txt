The schema describes a structured dance. It is an object type. It defines several reusable components under 'definitions': 'DanceMove' is an object or string, representing text of the dance element or providing a detailed structure with 'move' (string, the text of the dance element) and 'duration' (number, the length of the element). 'DanceSection' is an object, representing a section of dance elements with a required 'label' (string, e.g. A, A1, B2) and 'elements' (array, containing items that are DanceMove). 'DanceTune' is an object, providing information about the dance tune with optional properties: 'title' (string, the title of the dance tune), 'author' (string, author of the dance tune, if known), 'key' (string, the key of the dance tune), 'meter' (string, the meter of the dance tune), and 'source' (string, source of the dance tune). The main properties of the schema are: 'title' is a string, required, describing the title of the dance; 'author' is a string, optional, describing who wrote the dance, if known; 'genre' is a string, required, describing the dance genre such as 'Contra Dance' or 'English Country Dance'; 'formation' is a string, optional, describing the dance formation; 'source' is a string, optional, describing the source for the dance; 'date' is a string, optional, describing the date of composition of the dance; 'authors_notes' is a string, optional, describing notes about the dance written by the dance author; 'moves' is an array, required, consisting of items that are DanceSection; 'tune' is an object, optional, referencing the DanceTune definition. No additional properties are allowed at the root level.
