The schema describes SMS messaging configuration. It is an object type. It defines several reusable components under 'definitions': 'accountSid' is a string, it must have a minimum length of 1. 'authToken' is a string, it must have a minimum length of 1. 'toNumbers' is an array, it must contain at least 1 item of type string. 'fromNumber' is a string, it must have a minimum length of 1. 'messagingServiceSid' is a string, it must have a minimum length of 1. 'bodyTemplate' is a string, it must have a minimum length of 1. 'mediaUrl' is a string, it must have a minimum length of 1 and must be in URI format. 'maxPrice' is a string. The main properties of the schema are: The schema requires either the first configuration or the second configuration in 'oneOf'. The first configuration requires 'accountSid', 'authToken', 'toNumbers', and 'fromNumber', and at least one of 'bodyTemplate' or 'mediaUrl'. The second configuration requires 'accountSid', 'authToken', 'toNumbers', and 'messagingServiceSid', and at least one of 'bodyTemplate' or 'mediaUrl'. No additional properties are allowed at the root level.
