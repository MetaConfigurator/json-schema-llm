The schema describes allowances. It is an object type. The main properties of the schema are: 'annualInvestmentAllowance' is a number that can be claimed on equipment bought (except cars) up to maximum annual amount, optional; it must be a multiple of 0.01 and a minimum of 0. 'businessPremisesRenovationAllowance' is a number for the assisted areas only, optional; it must be a multiple of 0.01 and a minimum of 0. 'costOfReplacingDomesticItems' is a number for the cost of replacing domestic items, optional; it must be a multiple of 0.01 and a minimum of 0. 'zeroEmissionsGoodsVehicleAllowance' is a number for the zero emissions goods vehicle allowance, optional; it must be a multiple of 0.01 and a minimum of 0. 'otherCapitalAllowance' is a number for all other capital allowances, optional; it must be a multiple of 0.01 and a minimum of 0. No additional properties are allowed at the root level.
