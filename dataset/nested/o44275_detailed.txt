The schema describes an instance of a hotspot transform position. It is an object type. The main properties of the schema are: 'phi' is a number that specifies the position on the vertical axis in degrees, with a minimum of -90 and a maximum of 90; 'radius' is a number indicating the radius, i.e., the distance between the camera and the hotspot, with a minimum of 0 and a maximum of 1000; 'theta' is a number representing the position on the horizontal axis in degrees, with a minimum of 0 and a maximum of 360; 'x' is a number that indicates the position on the X axis of the world, with a minimum of -10000 and a maximum of 10000; 'y' is a number specifying the position on the Y axis of the world, with a minimum of -10000 and a maximum of 10000; 'z' is a number that indicates the position on the Z axis of the world, with a minimum of -10000 and a maximum of 10000. No additional properties are allowed at the root level.
