The schema describes allowances. It is an object type. The main properties of the schema are: 'annualInvestmentAllowance' is a number representing the annual investment allowance on items that qualify up to the AIA amount. The value must be between 0 and 99999999999.99 and is optional with a multiple of 0.01. 'businessPremisesRenovationAllowance' is a number representing the business premises renovation allowance for converting or renovating unused qualifying business premises. The value must be between 0 and 99999999999.99 and is optional with a multiple of 0.01. 'capitalAllowanceMainPool' is a number representing capital allowances at 18% on equipment, including cars with lower CO2 emissions. The value must be between 0 and 99999999999.99 and is optional with a multiple of 0.01. 'zeroEmissionGoodsVehicleAllowance' is a number representing the zero emission goods vehicle allowance for goods vehicles purchased for business use. The value must be between 0 and 99999999999.99 and is optional with a multiple of 0.01. 'capitalAllowanceSpecialRatePool' is a number representing capital allowances at 8% on equipment, including cars with higher CO2 emissions. The value must be between 0 and 99999999999.99 and is optional with a multiple of 0.01. 'enhancedCapitalAllowance' is a number representing 100% and other enhanced capital allowances. The value must be between 0 and 99999999999.99 and is optional with a multiple of 0.01. 'allowanceOnSales' is a number representing allowances on sale or cessation of business use where assets are disposed of for less than their tax value. The value must be between 0 and 99999999999.99 and is optional with a multiple of 0.01. 'capitalAllowanceSingleAssetPool' is a number representing capital allowances for single asset pool costs. The value must be between 0 and 99999999999.99 and is optional with a multiple of 0.01. 'tradingAllowance' is a number representing a tax exemption of a limited amount for individuals with trading income. The value must be between 0 and 99999999999.99 and is optional with a multiple of 0.01. No additional properties are allowed at the root level.
