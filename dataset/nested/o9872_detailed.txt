The schema describes a structure for managing experience domains. It is an object type. The main properties of the schema are: 'items' is an array containing schemas for individual experience domains, where each domain must have 'id', 'experienceDomainId', and 'applicationId' as strings matching the pattern '^[A-Fa-f\d]{24}$', and they may also include 'creationDate' and 'lastUpdated' as date-time formatted strings, 'sslCert' and 'sslBundle' as strings with a minimum length of 50 and a maximum length of 32767, 'domainName' as a string with a minimum length of 3 and a maximum length of 1024, and 'version' as a string with a minimum length of 1 and a maximum length of 255. 'count' is an integer. 'totalCount' is an integer. 'applicationId' is a string matching the pattern '^[A-Fa-f\d]{24}$'. No additional properties are allowed at the root level.
