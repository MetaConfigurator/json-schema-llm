It defines several reusable components under 'definitions': The root schema resolves to the defined capabilities. The schema requires at least one of the following capabilities: 'deliveryTypes', 'performanceAnalysisTypes', 'securityTypes', 'softwareDevelopmentTypes', 'supportAndOperationsTypes', 'testingAndAuditingTypes', 'userExperienceAndDesignTypes', or 'userResearchTypes', with their respective items.
