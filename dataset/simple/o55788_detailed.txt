The schema describes a measurement report provided by a CBSD. It is an object type. The main properties of the schema are: 'eutraCarrierRssiRpt' is an array report structured used by a CBSD when a measurement report type is EutraCarrierRssiNonTx or EutraCarrierRssiAlways. The items in the array are of type object and reference the 'eutraCarrierRssiRptObject'. The 'eutraCarrierRssiRptObject' is an object, report for a particular Carrier RSSI measurement. It has required fields: 'measFrequency', 'measBandwidth', and 'measCarrierRssi'. 'measFrequency' is a number, frequency of the low end of the measured channel in Hz, with a minimum of 0. 'measBandwidth' is a number, measurement bandwidth of the channel in Hz, with a minimum of 0. 'measCarrierRssi' is a number, the carrier RSSI measurement of the channel in dBm as per 3GPP TS 32.592, with a minimum of -100 and a maximum of -25. No additional properties are allowed at the root level.
