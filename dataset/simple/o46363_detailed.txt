The schema describes the structure for updating dividends income. It is an object type. The main properties of the schema are: 'ukDividends' is a number representing the total dividends payments received from UK companies, including dividends from employee share schemes. The value must be between 0 and 99999999999.99, with up to 2 decimal places. 'otherUkDividends' is a number representing dividends received from UK investment funds, including those from accumulation units. The value must be between 0 and 99999999999.99, with up to 2 decimal places. No additional properties are allowed at the root level.
