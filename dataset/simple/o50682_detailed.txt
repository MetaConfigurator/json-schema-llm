The schema describes deleting a credit card payment method from an account. It is an object type. The main properties of the schema are: 'customer' is a string representing the platform's customer, which is required and must have a minimum length of 1 and a maximum length of 96. 'account_identifier' is a string for the identifier of the platform account to fund, which is required, must have a minimum length of 5 and a maximum length of 96, and must match the pattern '^[\\+\\w-]{5,96}$'. 'cc_token' is a string for the payment token issued at registration time, which is required and must have a minimum length of 1 and a maximum length of 96. No additional properties are allowed at the root level.
