The schema describes minimum required output for an Email. It is an object type. It defines several reusable components under 'definitions': 'bodyPart' is an object, which can be either a singular body part with required properties for 'contentTransferEncoding', 'contentType', and 'rawContent', or a multipart type requiring 'contentTransferEncoding', 'contentType', and an array of 'bodyParts'. 'contact' is an object, which requires the field 'email', and may optionally include 'name'. The main properties of the schema are: 'to' is an array of contact, required with a minimum of 1 item. 'bodyParts' is an array of bodyPart, required with a minimum of 1 item. The schema conforms to two configurations via 'allOf': one requires 'from' as an array of contact with a minimum of 1 and maximum of 1 item, and an optional 'sender' that may be null or a contact; the other requires 'from' as an array of contact with a minimum of 2 items and a required 'sender' as contact. No additional properties are allowed at the root level.
