The schema describes a Digital Outcomes and Specialists 4 User research participants Service. It is an object type. The main properties of the schema are: 'anonymousRecruitment' is a boolean required field indicating whether the recruitment is anonymous. 'locations' is an array of strings required field, consisting of allowed values: 'Scotland', 'North East England', 'North West England', 'Yorkshire and the Humber', 'East Midlands', 'West Midlands', 'East of England', 'Wales', 'London', 'South East England', 'South West England', 'Northern Ireland', 'International (outside the UK)', with a minimum of 1 and a maximum of 13 unique items. 'manageIncentives' is a boolean required field indicating if incentives should be managed. 'recruitFromList' is a boolean required field indicating if recruitment should be from a list. 'recruitMethods' is an array of strings required field, consisting of allowed values: 'Entirely offline', 'Initial recruitment offline, but then contact them online', 'Entirely online', with a minimum of 1 and a maximum of 3 unique items. No additional properties are allowed at the root level.
