The schema describes a savings account source of income. It is an object type. The main properties of the schema are: 'accountName' is a string representing a friendly account name. It is required and must be unique for that National Insurance number. The account name must be between 1 and 32 characters in length and can only include mixed case alphanumeric characters and the characters; &'()*,-./@£. The pattern for this property is '^[A-Za-z0-9 &'\(\)\*,\-\./@£]{1,32}$'. No additional properties are allowed at the root level.
