The schema describes data about different subtitle encodings and confidences of auto-transcribed content. It is an object type. It defines several reusable components under 'definitions': 'subtitle_url' is an object, which contains properties: 'format' is a string, the format of the subtitles (e.g. SRT, DFXP, WEB_VTT, etc) and 'url' is a string, the url of the subtitle stream. The main properties of the schema are: 'confidence' is a number, how confident the transcriber (human or automated) is of the accuracy of the subtitles (optional); 'urls' is an array, the locations of any subtitle transcriptions of the video (optional) with items that must adhere to the 'subtitle_url' definition. No additional properties are allowed at the root level.
