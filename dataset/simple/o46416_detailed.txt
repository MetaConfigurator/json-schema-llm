The schema describes an expense record. It is an object type. The main properties of the schema are: 'amount' is a number representing expenses associated with the running of the business. It is required and must be between -99999999999.99 and 99999999999.99 with a multiple of 0.01. 'disallowableAmount' is a number representing any expense or partial expense that cannot be claimed for tax purposes. It is optional and must also be between -99999999999.99 and 99999999999.99 with a multiple of 0.01. No additional properties are allowed at the root level.
