The schema describes a brief clarification question. It is an object type. The main properties of the schema are: 'question' is a string that is required and must have a minimum length of 1 and a maximum length of 5000, adhering to the pattern '^$|(^(?:\S+\s+){0,99}\S+$)'. 'answer' is a string that is required and must have a minimum length of 1 and a maximum length of 5000, also adhering to the pattern '^$|(^(?:\S+\s+){0,99}\S+$)'. No additional properties are allowed at the root level.
